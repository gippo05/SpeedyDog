Reminders for Online Booking passengers

For a very convenient experience for online booking passengers, remember to follow these guidelines.

  1. Please be at the port at least 2 hours prior the time of departure and proceed to the vessel boarding area.
  2. Prepare the printed e-ticket for check-in.
  3. Strictly follow the day and time of departure from the e-ticket.
  4. Notice of admin fee and rolling cargo fee is non-refundable.

Have a very safe, very fast, and very convenient trip!
